About Zardo Lab

Thoughtful.
Experimental.
Hospitable.

Zardo Lab is a space where indulgence becomes intentional — measured, crafted with care, shared generously, and enjoyed together. Inspired by treasured memories of cottage days on the island where everyone is welcome, travels to some of the world's best cocktail bars, and evenings at home.

We don't drink to forget;
we drink to remember.
