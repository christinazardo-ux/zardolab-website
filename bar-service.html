Cocktail Experience

Private Cocktail
Experiences

Thoughtful cocktails for small, elevated gatherings. Zardo Lab offers private cocktail service for intimate events, typically 6 to 20 guests, where the drinks are part of the experience — not an afterthought.

This is not high-volume mixed-drink service. If you're looking for fast rum-and-Cokes for a crowd, I may not be your person. But if you want a small cocktail menu made with care, proper glassware, beautiful garnishes, thoughtful flavours, and just the right amount of delightful excess — that is very much the Zardo Lab zone.

Get in Touch
01

Private Cocktail Service

For small events where cocktails are prepared and served on-site. I can bring the bar tools, mixers, garnishes, glassware, ice, supplies, and ingredients needed to create a polished cocktail experience.

I just need access to a sink and a bar, counter, or serving table.

02

Batch Cocktails

For hosts who want something beautiful, delicious, and ready to serve. Batch cocktails can be prepared in advance with custom menus, garnish kits, serving instructions, and everything you need to pour confidently.

Perfect for dinner parties, cottage weekends, birthdays, pre-wedding gatherings, or a very well-stocked fridge.

03

Custom Cocktail Menus

For events that need a signature drink or a small curated menu. Cocktails can be designed around the occasion, season, theme, favourite flavours, or the general mood you want to create.

How It Works

The process

01 Tell me what you're planning: the date, location, guest count, mood, and anything you already know you like.
02 I'll help shape the right format — on-site cocktail service, prepared batch cocktails, a custom menu, or a mix of all three.
03 Once the details are clear, I'll provide a custom quote based on the size, location, ingredients, supplies, and complexity of the service.

What I need
from you

Access to a sink
A bar, counter, or serving table
A clear setup area
Event timing and guest count
Any venue rules or alcohol service requirements
A sense of the mood, flavours, or experience you want

Pricing

Every event is quoted individually. Pricing depends on guest count, location, service format, cocktail complexity, ingredients, alcohol needs, glassware, ice, garnishes, and additional supplies.

Responsible Service

Zardo Lab is about measured indulgence: memorable drinks, thoughtful service, and a good night people actually remember fondly.

Depending on the event location and format, alcohol service requirements may vary. We can discuss what is needed during planning so the event is set up properly and responsibly.
